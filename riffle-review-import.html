Tips for accuracy
Copy the review text exactly as written — don't paraphrase. Use the reviewer's actual name or initials if they've posted publicly. If you're entering from a private email, use first name + last initial only. Approximate dates are fine.
How your reviews will appear on Riffle
This is exactly how your imported reviews will appear on your public Riffle profile. The source badge and "Imported" label are always visible to anglers. Riffle-verified bookings (trips completed through Riffle) will appear in a separate section above these.
Before you import
Riffle takes review integrity seriously. By importing, you're confirming these are genuine reviews from real clients. Imported reviews are clearly labeled so anglers always know which reviews are Riffle-verified and which come from elsewhere.
What happens after you submit: Your imported reviews go into a brief review queue. Riffle will add them to your profile within 24 hours. You'll get an email confirmation. You can add more reviews any time from your guide dashboard.
